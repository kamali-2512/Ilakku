Why Volunteer or Intern with Ilakku?

Volunteering and interning with Ilakku is a simple yet powerful way to support first-

generation learners and young women on their journey toward education, confidence,

and leadership. While there are countless reasons to join us, here are our top three:

1. Be a Changemaker

🎔 Your time and skills whether in teaching, mentoring, research, or behind-the-scenes support directly help young women break barriers and build brighter futures.

2. Grow with Purpose

🎔 Volunteering or interning with us is not just about giving; it’s about gaining. You’ll develop invaluable professional skills, leadership qualities, and community experience that will stay with you for life.

3. Belong to a Movement

🎔 No matter who you are or where you come from, there’s a space for you at Ilakku. With opportunities ranging from student mentoring to program support, you’ll be part of a growing community that uplifts and inspires.
