4 Years of Empowering Young Futures

From first steps in learning to confident leaders of tomorrow transforming education into opportunity, building confidence, and shaping brighter futures. ILAKKU was founded in 2021 with a vision to empower first-generation learners and young women through education, leadership, and opportunity. In just 4 years, we have created a safe space where students can learn, grow, and lead with confidence. What began as a small initiative has now blossomed into a thriving Leadership Academy in Madurai and Ramnad, reaching over 218 students, with more than 190 alumnae now employed. Through scholarships, skill-building, workshops, and community engagement, ILAKKU continues to ensure brighter, happier futures for young students determined to break barriers and build change.

Who We Are

ILAKKU (Reg. No: 19/2021) is a registered trust, dedicated to empower underprivileged women by enhancing their access to education, career opportunities, and holistic development. We provide scholarships for first-generation learners, market-relevant skill training, career guidance and our contributions are at various levels that provide a helping hand for the students enrolled over here. ILAKKU especially supports young women in building sustainable livelihoods, while fostering leadership and resilience. The organization also extends relief through medical and urgent assistance, addressing both immediate needs and long-term empowerment. Founded by Mr. Ariaravelan, Ilakku continues to grow as a supportive network that nurtures personal growth, independence, and positive community impact.
