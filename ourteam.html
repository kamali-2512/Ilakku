OUR TEAM.

At Ilakku, our team is made up of passionate educators, mentors, and community leaders who believe in the power of education to transform lives. We work closely with students, parents, and local communities to provide guidance, resources, and encouragement every step of the way. Each member brings unique expertise from teaching and leadership training to career mentoring and counselling ensuring that our students receive holistic support. What unites us is a shared commitment to empowering first-generation learners and young women to break barriers and achieve their dreams. Together, we strive to build a culture of care, resilience, and opportunity that goes beyond classrooms and into communities.

Profile

K. Ariaravelan brings over three decades of rich experience in training youth, rural women, social workers, and educators. His expertise spans social analysis, gender equity, financial management, life skills development, and pedagogy.

He is the architect of a comprehensive life skills education program designed for children and adolescents, which has been adopted by the School Education Departments across 17 states in India. The program continues to be actively implemented in schools, making a meaningful contribution to holistic student development.

Currently, he is engaged in developing a similar life skills curriculum tailored specifically for college students, further extending his impact on youth education.

In addition to his work in training and education, Ariaravelan is a prolific writer. His published works include Ooratchiyum Pengalum, Vethi Vazhvuum Sindhanaikum, Ula, and Vaazhthu Paar, which reflect his deep insights into social development and human values.

Profile
